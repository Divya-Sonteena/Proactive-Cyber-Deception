How It Works

The platform continuously feeds live honeypot traffic through an AI pipeline to classify, score, and guide response.

🍯

Honeypot

Cowrie (SSH) and Dionaea (multi-protocol) honeypots capture real attacker sessions and traffic.

→
🔤

Tokenization

Raw events are converted to canonical tokens — SCAN, LOGIN_ATT, EXEC, FILE_XFER — forming attack sequences.

→
🧠

ML Models

DistilBERT classifies attacks (95.6% accuracy). XLNet predicts next attacker action from sequence context.

→
⚠

Risk Scoring

Combined severity score (token severity + attack probability + anomaly score) assigns LOW / MEDIUM / HIGH / CRITICAL risk.

→
🛡

Prevention

Structured mitigation guidance — Immediate, Short-term, Long-term — across access control, network security, and host hardening.
